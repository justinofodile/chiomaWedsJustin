Story

Our Love Story

First Meet

12 Sept 2021

On a typical Sunday afternoon, I unexpectedly joined a coursemate from NIIT, Hauwa, to deliver birthday items to Beely (picture) at the NYSC camp, Kubwa. In a casual and unremarkable first encounter, I met her. She wasn't particularly interested in the conversation. She was itchy to leave. But from that first interaction, I felt something different. It wasn’t just about talking; I knew deep down that this was more than just a chance encounter. I had a feeling, one that I couldn’t shake, that we were meant to be in each other’s lives... Justin

Dating

01 Oct 2021

On the 1st of October, he didn’t waste time at all as he made it clear that he wasn’t here to joke. He looked at me and said, "I’m serious about this. I know what I want, and I’m not here to waste your time." Just like that, he declared his intentions. And the truth is, I already had my own convictions. From that day, line upon line, a little here, a little there, we knew we were building something real. Chioma

Proposal

07 Sept 2023

The proposal wasn’t flashy or dramatic, but it was perfect for us. We had already gone to see each other’s parents. My mum accepted him immediately, but my dad? Well, he took his time! but eventually, he gave us his approval. After all the family formalities, one evening, he said, “Bring those your chingili fingers.” Before I could even react, he slid the ring on my finger. Just like that! No stress, no big scene, but the love in that moment was everything. Our families' acceptance made the proposal feel even more special. It wasn’t just about us anymore; it was about two families coming together. This phase felt even more significant because we had the support and blessings of spiritual authorities over us and those we hold dear. Chioma

A Journey of Commitment

We had initially planned to have our church wedding soon after the engagement, but as we say in Nigeria, “life happened.” There were a lot of delays, back-and-forth, and moments of frustration. But through it all, it was clear that the journey we had started together was to the audience of One. The delays only made us stronger, patient, and more committed to this union. Now, as we prepare to finally celebrate our wedding, this scripture stands tall in our hearts: Its roots may grow old in the ground and its stump die in the soil, yet at the scent of water it will bud and put forth shoots like a plant. Job 14: 8-9
